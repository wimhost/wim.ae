Museum Of The Future

Interactive Solutions in the Museum Of The Future

The project

For an event at the @museumofthefuture, we were asked to come up with interactive solutions. The company had a lot of content and wanted to share it with their customer at the event. They wanted to present their content in a way that was visual, understandable and engaging. We came up with two solutions: our Touch Oled Screen, which is an interactive transparent display, with our LED wall combination and our six Displax Object-Recognition tables.

The Touch Oled Screen can display different categories for customer to choose from. By means of a simple drag and drop they can place the content on the large LED wall and see everything, from text to videos, that is shown in that category. Because the Oled Screen is an Interactive Transparent Display, it gives an extra dimension to your presentation.

Our variety of Object-Recognition tables are great for displaying multiple subjects/categories simultaneously. Visitors decide which category they are interested in and simply place it on the table to start rotating the object. All content in that category appears instantly such as text, infographs, photos, maps, videos, galleries, etc.

These are just a few examples of what we can do with our tangible products. If you have any questions or requests, please do not hesitate to contact us and together we will find the best immersive solution for you and your company.
