Sliding Screen

Sliding Screen

This sliding screen can just operate while the audience is passing by. The on-board obstruction recognition technology allows the screen to slow down and stop if anybody is in its path. Controllable by I-pad or automated sensor technology. The right alignment with the background creates a perfect blend between the analogue and digital world.
